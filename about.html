The Philosophy
01
THE DESIGNERR NAME
Not a typo. Not a brand trick. A philosophy. Likewise mathematics — I didn't learn calculus by reading it, I learned it by doing it wrong first. Every error in design is a data point, not a failure. That's why I build my identity around the mistakes — and call myself a DesignErr.
02
MATHS MAKES
BETTER DESIGN
My B.Sc. Mathematics background is not a detour — it's the foundation. Grid systems are geometry. Type scales are logarithmic progressions. Colour ratios are applied psychology. Most designers feel these things intuitively. I can explain exactly why they work.
03
SYSTEMS OVER
AESTHETICS
A logo is not a brand. A post is not a campaign. I build systems — visual identities that scale, motion languages that communicate, and print collateral that maintains consistency without a designer in the room. The goal is always the system, not the single deliverable.
04
STORYTELLING
IS THE SKILL
Brand design, motion graphics, a 3D animated feature film — these are all the same discipline: visual storytelling. The medium changes. The goal doesn't. Make someone feel something before they've read a single word.
