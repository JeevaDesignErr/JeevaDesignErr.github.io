About Me

Designer with a Maths
Foundation

Likewise mathematics — I've studied design by making mistakes (errors) myself. So I wouldn't mind calling myself "DesignErr" — because every human learns through errors, not around them.

My B.Sc. Mathematics background gives me a systematic, logic-driven approach — grids are geometry, colour is psychology mapped with precision.
